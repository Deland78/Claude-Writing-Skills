# Story Arc: The Dead Zone Signal

## Arc Overview
Marcus Reeves, a former military medic surviving by trade in the post-collapse Eastern Corridor, receives a coded signal from Zone 3 using his dead unit's encryption. His journey to answer the signal forces him through hostile territory, into an uneasy alliance with Elena Vasquez (a woman with her own reasons to reach Zone 3), and ultimately to a confrontation with the Corridor's most dangerous faction leader — who wants what Marcus's CO died to protect. The arc traces Marcus from isolation to reluctant trust to betrayal to a final choice between self-preservation and the vulnerability required to rebuild something worth protecting.

## Act Progression

### Act 1: Setup / Beginning Hook (0–25%)
Marcus's routine is established: safe, isolated, controlled. The signal disrupts everything. He resists, then discovers evidence that his CO's secret is real and time-sensitive. He commits to the journey. Meets Elena at the Corridor border. Trust is immediately tested.

### Act 2A: Complication / Rising Action (25–50%)
Marcus and Elena navigate Zone 3's outer ring. They encounter faction scouts, a decaying urban landscape, and remnants of Marcus's old unit. Midpoint reversal: they find the signal's source — but it's a relay, not the origin. The real destination is deeper in the dead zone, and someone has been tracking them.

### Act 2B: Crisis / Escalation (50–75%)
Resources dwindle. Elena's hidden agenda surfaces — she's connected to the faction leader. Marcus must decide whether her information is genuine or a trap. A third party (Dex, a former comrade) appears with conflicting intelligence. The faction closes in. Marcus loses his medical pack — his identity and livelihood.

### Act 3: Climax / Resolution (75–100%)
Marcus reaches the CO's cache: not a weapon but a seed bank and medical archive — the tools to rebuild, not to dominate. The faction leader arrives. Marcus must choose: destroy the cache to keep it from the faction, or trust Elena's plan to distribute it across enclaves. He chooses trust. Cost: Dex dies covering their escape. The cache reaches three enclaves. Marcus stays in Zone 3 to rebuild.

## Thematic Pressure Points

| Act | Thematic Test | How Theme Is Pressured |
|-----|--------------|----------------------|
| Act 1 | Marcus refuses partnership | Self-preservation feels rational — trust is a liability |
| Act 2A | Marcus relies on Elena's knowledge | Small trust pays off, but vulnerability grows |
| Act 2B | Elena's connection to Harlan exposed | Trust seems to have been a mistake — betrayal feels confirmed |
| Act 3 | Marcus chooses to distribute the cache | Vulnerability wins — trust is rebuilt through action, not certainty |
